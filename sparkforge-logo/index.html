Why Concept 1 Is Recommended

The Modular Anvil + Spark best captures SparkForge's brand essence -- a tool that forges ideas into products.

  • Directly embodies the "Forge" metaphor with the anvil shape
  • The { } base connects to the developer audience instantly
  • Geometric simplicity works at any size (favicon to billboard)
  • Quench orange spark creates a strong focal point on any background
  • Unique silhouette -- won't be confused with Linear, Vercel, or GitHub
  • Adaptable: works square (icon), horizontal (header), or standalone (splash)
